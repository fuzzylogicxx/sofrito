Sofrito

A savoury base for front-end web development.

Sofrito is a starting point for responsive websites, made from quality ingredients:

  • semantic HTML,
  • modern CSS, and
  • vanilla JavaScript.

Get Started

Principles

Accessible from the ground up. Lean and fast. Progressively Enhanced to be simultaneously resilient and cutting edge. Non-reliant on JavaScript. User experience valued over developer convenience.

Techniques

Packed full of best practices and tricks tried and tested in commercial web development. Lots of typographic detail.

Patterns

Packed with solutions to common front-end challenges: SVG icons, sticky footers, accessible forms, modals, tabs, navigation and more.
