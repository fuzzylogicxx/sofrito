Sofrito

A tasty base for front-end web development.

Sofrito is a starting point for responsive websites, made from quality ingredients:

  • semantic HTML,
  • modern CSS, and
  • vanilla JavaScript.

Download v1.0

Principles

Accessible from the ground up. Lean and fast. Separation of structural, presentation and behavioural layers. Progressively enhanced to be simultaneously resilient and cutting edge. Non-reliant on JavaScript. User experience valued over developer convenience.

Techniques

Packed full of best practices and developer insights for accessibility, performance, and browser optimisation which have been tried and tested on commercial web projects. Lots of typographic detail. Sofrito stands on the shoulders of giants.

Patterns

Loaded with solutions to common front-end challenges: SVG icons, sticky footers, accessible forms, modals, tabs, navigation and more.
